Asthmart

A smart sensor that will make your asthma inhaler a source of unique information,thus, improving the life of asthmatics.

1. Monitor your asthma

Asthmart allows you to monitor your asthma and informs you when the dosage of the inhaler is nearly finished.

3. Analyze your surrounding

Asthmart contains a sensor that informs if the environmental conditions are favorable or not for asthmatics in a specific area.

2. Localise your inhaler

Asthmart allows you to localise your inhaler using GPS in case of loss and makes use of a sound sensor to find it easily.
